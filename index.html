CHANGELOG

12.29.2018

iOS Public Beta / Android Public Beta

We launched the public beta!!! Our closed beta allowed us to plug a few major holes before we opened it up to the world. Other than getting the data out, our biggest focuses were on performance, stability, and usability. Since we are in beta, feedback is paramount, so we opened our Discord channel to the public.


What's New?

  • Fixed a bug where Anna, Lei, and Noctis were not appearing in the Character Select
  • Updated frame properties for all characters
  • Added loading spinners to gif page while gifs are loading
  • Improved spacing on the Spreadsheet View
  • Added higher-quality Chicken portraits
  • Improved performance and bug fixes

What's Next?

  • Add special properties of about ten characters
  • Filters and ad removal are still in progress
  • Continue to work on the spreadsheet
  • Continue to improve performance and squash bugs


12.17.2018

iOS Closed Beta / Android Closed Beta

We did a very small rollout of the T7Chicken PLUS Beta on iOS and Android to prepare us for any issues that might come up with future, larger-scale releases.


What's New?

  • List view and Thumbnail view on the Character Select
  • List view and Spreadsheet View on the Move List
  • The ability the mark favorite characters and moves
  • Gifs for moves that have them

What's Next?

  • Filters on the Move List
  • More work on the Spreadsheet View
  • Ad Removal / Donation functionality
  • Completing the new in-house character data
  • Polish and bug fixing
